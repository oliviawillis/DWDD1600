About Me

I am 22 years old and the eldest of 5 children in my family. I grew up in Draper, Utah and attended Corner Canyon High School. I was that kid who didn't want to be noticed. I worked really hard in school in the hopes that I would get into BYU. I will never forget the way I felt the day I opened my letter of acceptance into BYU.

Since highschool, I've attended multiple colleges and learned a lot about myself in the process.

  1. One year at BYU
  2. One year at USU
  3. 3 months in Alcala de Henares in Spain for a study aborad learning spanish
  4. Two years at UVU

I began my journey interested in psychology, but realized that I was more interested in reading about psychology than having a career in that line of work. I eventually decided to combine my love for psychology and buisness and found myself in digital marketing. After attending UVU for about 2 semesters, I applied to work at Green House Sales and Marketing. Green House has given me so much opportunity and growth. I find myself excited to go to work because I know I will leave knowing that I learned something new that day. I like to say that I attend UVU for Green House, not the classes.

This last August 2024, I got married to my best friend in the Draper Utah Temple. Immediately following my honeymoon, I was promoted to both Project Manager and Hiring and Onboarding Specialist. Now I am finishing my last year of college, set to graduate in May 2025, and I am excited for what the future holds.
